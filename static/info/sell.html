How do I sell a stock?

To sell shares of a certain stock you place an offer to sell (ASK) on a stock (player) that your anaimal insticts (analysis) tells you is going to improve, at your desired price and quantity of shares. Once a buy offer (BID) meets your price, a trade is made between you and the buyer. The trade is made at the ask price, and at the minimal quantity of the two offers.

e.g if a bid of 10 shares at 420 cents meets an ask of 21 shares at 412 cents, the trade is made at 412 cents and 10 shares are traded.
