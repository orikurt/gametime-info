How do I buy a stock?

You purchase shares of a stock by placing an offer to buy (BID) on a stock at your desired price and quantity of shares. Once your bid is processed and accepted, it is pending a counter sell offer (ASK). Once an ask that meets your price is placed, a trade is made between you and the seller. The trade is made at the ask price, and at the minimal quantity of the two offers.

e.g if a bid of 10 shares at 420 cents meets an ask of 21 shares at 412 cents, the trade is made at 412 cents and 10 shares are traded.
