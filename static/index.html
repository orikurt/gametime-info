How can I buy a stock?

To purchase shares of a stock you place a bid on a stock (player) that your anaimal insticts (analysis) tells you is going to improve, at your desired price and quantity of shares. Once a sell offer (ASK) meets your price, a trade is made between you and the seller

Learn More

How can I sell a stock?

To sell shares of a certain stock you place an offer to sell (ASK) on a stock (player) that your anaimal insticts (analysis) tells you is going to improve, at your desired price and quantity of shares. Once a buy offer (BID) meets your price, a trade is made between you and the buyer

Learn More

How do trades work?

GameTime Market Trades work just like those in the stock market. When an offer to buy (BID) meets an offer to sell (ASK) - a trade is made between the parties involved

Learn More

How are prices set?

The prices are set, just as in the stock market, according to the latest trade made on the stock, and thus indicate the market's current valuation of the stock

Learn More

How are players valuated?

Players stock value is calculated by aggregating the players performance over the course of his career. Individual performance and team success contribute to market value.

Learn More

What happens when a player retires?

When a players retires, shareholders can either convert their shares into draft picks according to the player's final aggregated value, or credit their account with the cash value of their shares.

What are the market opening hours?

The market schedule is as follows:
- Opening stage
- Trade stage
- Closing stage
- Closed
* Times are in UTC (GMT +0)

What do the different stages mean?

An offer can be made and nominal prices are updated in real time, but no trades are made until the market opens, where all deals in queue are executed

Regular trading stage. Trades are made immediately upon availability

An offer can be made and nominal prices are updated in real time, but no trades are made until the closing time where all deals in queue are executed

No offers or deals are accepted
